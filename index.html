NOAH (Niche Object Affiliate & Handling)

It is an innovative online marketplace designed exclusively for students of the Technological Institute of the Philippines (T.I.P.).

Our platform enables students to buy and sell used books, school materials, and essential academic resources within the school community.

Whether you're looking to pass on your old textbooks, find affordable supplies, or connect with fellow students for academic needs, NOAH is the perfect place for you!
